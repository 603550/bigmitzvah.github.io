Scope of Work: 2024 Ram ProMaster

Vehicle: 2024 Ram ProMaster (High Roof / Short Wheelbase)
Reference Consultant: Lauren Lawliss

NOTE TO GC: The videos below are for technique/method. Please follow the written specifications below each video for material changes (specifically floor insulation) and fastener sizes.

1. Floor & Subfloor

⚠️ CRITICAL DEVIATION FROM VIDEO

The video shows a "floating" floor. DO NOT DO THIS. We must frame the floor to accommodate insulation.

  • Prep: Treat any exposed metal with Rustoleum Clean Metal Primer + Enamel.
  • Framing: Install wooden slats (furring strips) lengthwise along the floor valleys. Secure with Sikaflex 252 or bolts.
  • Insulation: Cut XPS Rigid Foam Board (Pink/Blue board) to fit tightly between slats.
    (Do NOT use Thinsulate on the floor).
  • Scribing: Use the "Tick Stick" method shown at 07:25 in the video to scribe subfloor to curves.
  • Fastening: Screw subfloor into wooden slats, not the metal floor.

2. Walls & Framing

Technique: "Square Out" Method

As shown in the video, frame walls to square out the bottom third (up to lower horizontal rib) for easier cabinetry installation.

HARDWARE SPECS (PLUS NUTS):
• Standard Hex Holes: 1/4-20 Thread Plus Nuts
• Large Round Factory Holes: 10-32 Thread Plus Nuts
• Note: Use Plus Nuts (Cross Nuts) only. Do not use standard Rivnuts.
  • Framing Grid: Bolt horizontal strips to Plus Nuts. Use Kreg Pocket Holes for vertical supports (see video).
  • Insulation: Stuff TWO LAYERS of 3M Thinsulate behind panels.

3. Ceiling

  • Furring Strips: Use 1/2-inch Plywood ripped into 2.5"–3" strips.
    (Do not use 1x3 lumber; it is too stiff to curve).
  • Fasteners: Secure using 5/16" Plus Nuts (approx 5 per rib).
  • Cladding: Cedar Tongue & Groove, blind nailed into the plywood strips.
  • Venting: NO ROOF FANS. Run ceiling continuous.

4. Electrical Rough-In

  • Routing: Run all wiring EXTERNALLY (inside future bench/cabinet bases). Avoid burying wires behind finished walls.
  • Battery Loc: Rough-in battery location at the REAR (near back doors).
  • Lighting: Pre-wire ceiling puck lights before T&G installation. Leave long tails.
